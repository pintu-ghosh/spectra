Hi, I’m Pintu Ghosh, a physicist specializing in nanophotonics, light–matter interactions, and metasurfaces. My research explores how light behaves at the nanoscale, aiming to push the limits of optical functionality through theory, simulation, and experiments. I am currently an Associate Professor at the College of Optical Science and Engineering, Zhejiang University, in Hangzhou, China.
